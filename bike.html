Benifits of Bike delivery

1. Service:
       Bikes can swiftly maneuver through traffic and narrow streets, ensuring quick deliveries, especially in congested urban areas.

2. Environmentally Friendly:
       Bikes produce zero emissions, making them an eco-friendly option that reduces carbon footprint and promotes sustainable transportation.

3. Cost-Effective:
       Operating and maintaining bikes for delivery is typically more affordable than cars or trucks, allowing businesses to offer competitive pricing to customers.

4. Healthy Lifestyle:
       Delivery riders get exercise while working, contributing to a healthier lifestyle compared to sedentary delivery methods, which can improve overall well-being.

5. Accessibility:
      Bikes can access areas where cars might struggle, such as pedestrian zones or areas with limited parking, ensuring accessibility to customers in diverse locations.
