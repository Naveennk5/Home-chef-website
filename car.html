Benifits of Car delivery

1. Capacity for Larger Orders:
       Cars have more space than bikes, enabling the delivery of larger orders or multiple orders in one trip, catering to families or groups.

2. Weather Protection:
       Cars offer protection from adverse weather conditions, ensuring that food stays fresh and intact during transportation, regardless of rain or extreme temperatures.

3. Comfortable and Convenient:
       Customers can expect a more comfortable delivery experience, as cars provide a controlled environment with seating and space for the delivery personnel.

4. Extended Range:
       Cars can cover longer distances than bikes, making them suitable for suburban areas or locations further away from the restaurant or kitchen.

5. Versatility:
       Cars can be used for both food delivery and ride-sharing services, providing businesses with additional revenue streams and flexibility in operations.
